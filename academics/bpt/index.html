Bachelor of Physiotherapy (B.P.T)

A 4.5-year comprehensive journey to becoming a skilled healthcare professional with a 6-month compulsory clinical internship.

Program Overview

The Bachelor of Physiotherapy (B.P.T) at SNS College of Physiotherapy is designed to provide students with an in-depth understanding of human anatomy, physiology, and the therapeutic practices required for physical rehabilitation. Our curriculum is uniquely enhanced with Design Thinking, enabling students to innovate patient care.

Students gain hands-on experience through clinical postings in multi-specialty hospitals starting from their early years, ensuring they are well-prepared for real-world medical challenges.

Course Highlights

  • 4-Year Academic Study + 6 Months Internship
  • Clinical Training in Multi-specialty Hospitals
  • Advanced Labs & Modern Equipment
  • Research-oriented Skill Development
  • Design Thinking in Healthcare Practice
