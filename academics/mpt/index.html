Advanced Specialization

The Master of Physiotherapy (M.P.T) at SNS College of Physiotherapy offers students the opportunity to specialize in advanced fields like Orthopaedics, Neurology, and Paediatrics. Our PG program focuses on research, evidence-based clinical practice, and advanced therapeutic interventions.

With access to specialized clinics and advanced research labs, M.P.T students are prepared for leadership roles in clinical practice, research organizations, and academic institutions.

Specializations Offered

  • Orthopaedics
  • Neurology
  • Paediatrics
  • Research Methodology & Biostatistics
  • Advanced Clinical Skills
